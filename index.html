WHO WE ARE

Youth RedCross Circle, Thapathali Campus

The Youth RedCross Circle, Thapathali Campus (Youth RedCross Circle, Thapathali Campus) is a branch of Nepal Red Cross Society (NRCS) operating at Thapathali Campus. Founded with the mission to promote humanitarian values among youth, we empower students and young volunteers through service, education, and action.

Guided by the seven Fundamental Principles of the International Red Cross and Red Crescent Movement – Humanity, Impartiality, Neutrality, Independence, Voluntary Service, Unity, and Universality – Youth RedCross Circle, Thapathali Campus works to prevent and alleviate human suffering.
