Blood Donation

Youth RedCross Circle, Thapathali Campus runs one of the most active blood donation programs in Kathmandu. We organize regular blood donation camps at Thapathali Campus and coordinate with Nepal Red Cross Blood Bank to ensure safe blood supply to hospitals.

Our team of trained volunteers manages the entire process – from donor registration and health screening to blood collection and post-donation care – ensuring a safe and comfortable experience for every donor.

  • Weekly blood donation camps every Saturday, 9AM–1PM
  • Over 5,000 units collected since establishment
  • Coordination with NRCS Blood Bank, Kathmandu
  • Donor health screening and post-donation refreshments
  • Emergency blood requirement support 24/7

First Aid & Health Services

Our first aid training programs equip students and community members with lifesaving skills. We conduct basic and advanced first aid courses following NRCS and IFRC guidelines, certified by the Nepal Red Cross Society.

Youth RedCross Circle, Thapathali Campus also organizes free health camps in the Thapathali area, providing medical consultations, basic health checkups, and hygiene awareness to underprivileged communities.

  • Basic and Advanced First Aid certification courses
  • CPR and emergency response training
  • Quarterly free health camps in surrounding communities
  • Hygiene promotion and disease prevention awareness
  • Mental health first aid training for youth

Disaster Response

Nepal is one of the most disaster-prone countries in the world. Youth RedCross Circle, Thapathali Campus maintains a trained force of volunteers ready to respond to earthquakes, floods, landslides, and fires. Our team played a pivotal role in relief during the 2015 earthquake.

We conduct regular disaster preparedness drills, community awareness programs, and maintain an emergency response kit for rapid deployment. Our volunteers are trained in search and rescue, emergency shelter management, and psychosocial support.

  • Rapid response team on standby 24/7
  • Search and rescue operations training
  • Emergency shelter and relief distribution
  • Coordination with NRCS and government agencies
  • Community disaster risk reduction programs

Youth Empowerment

Youth are at the core of Youth RedCross Circle, Thapathali Campus's mission. We run a comprehensive youth empowerment program that includes leadership development, peer education, life skills training, and community engagement activities.

Our programs encourage critical thinking, teamwork, and compassionate leadership, preparing engineering students to be responsible citizens and future leaders who are committed to serving their communities.

  • Leadership and communication skills workshops
  • Peer education and mentoring programs
  • Environmental and tree plantation campaigns
  • Youth Red Cross unit meetings and activities
  • Participation in national and international Red Cross events

PROGRAM

Community Development

Youth RedCross Circle, Thapathali Campus goes beyond the campus to serve the wider Thapathali community. Our volunteers engage in regular cleanup campaigns, tree plantation drives, slum area support, and community awareness programs.

We believe that a healthy, safe, and educated community is the foundation of a peaceful society. Our community programs aim to address social issues, promote hygiene, and support marginalized groups in the area.

  • Monthly community cleanup campaigns
  • Tree plantation and environmental awareness
  • Support for elderly homes and orphanages
  • Women's health and empowerment programs
  • Scholarship support for underprivileged students
