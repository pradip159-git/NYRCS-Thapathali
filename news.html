BLOOD DONATION

NYRCST Blood Donation Camp Collects 200+ Units at Thapathali Campus

A successful blood donation drive organized by NYRCST at Thapathali Campus collected over 200 units of blood for Nepal Red Cross Blood Bank on May 10, 2025. More than 250 donors participated in the event.

May 15, 2025 NYRCST Team Events
TRAINING

First Aid Training Completed for 150 Engineering Students

NYRCST successfully conducted a comprehensive first aid training program for 150 engineering students of Thapathali Campus. The 3-day program covered CPR, wound management, and emergency response.

April 22, 2025 NYRCST Team Training
DISASTER RESPONSE

NYRCST Volunteers Participate in National Earthquake Preparedness Drill

More than 80 NYRCST volunteers participated in the national earthquake preparedness drill coordinated by NRCS and the Department of Disaster Management. The drill tested rapid response and evacuation procedures.

March 10, 2025 NYRCST Team Disaster
HEALTH

Free Health Camp Serves 300 Community Members in Thapathali

NYRCST organized a free health camp at Thapathali that provided basic health checkups, blood pressure monitoring, blood sugar testing, and hygiene awareness to over 300 community members, many from underprivileged backgrounds.

February 14, 2025 NYRCST Team Health
COMMUNITY

NYRCST Plants 500 Trees Around Thapathali Campus on Environment Day

On World Environment Day, NYRCST volunteers planted over 500 saplings around Thapathali Campus and surrounding areas in a community-wide plantation drive, contributing to urban greening in Kathmandu.

June 5, 2024 NYRCST Team Community
