WHY VOLUNTEER

Why Join YRC?

Volunteering with YRC is one of the most rewarding experiences you can have as a student.

Certified Training

Receive NRCS-certified training in first aid, disaster response, and leadership – skills that last a lifetime.

Community Impact

Make a real, measurable difference in the lives of people in your campus and surrounding community.

Career Development

Build your CV with meaningful volunteer experience and gain skills valued by employers worldwide.

Global Network

Connect with the world's largest humanitarian network – the International Red Cross and Red Crescent Movement.

Personal Growth

Develop empathy, leadership, teamwork, and communication skills through real-world humanitarian service.

Recognition

Outstanding volunteers are recognized and awarded at campus, national, and international levels.

HOW IT WORKS

Volunteer Registration Process

Joining YRC is simple. Fill out the registration form and our team will contact you within 3 working days to guide you through the orientation process.

1

Fill the Form

Complete the volunteer registration form with your personal details and areas of interest.

2

Orientation

Attend a YRC orientation session to learn about our organization, values, and programs.

3

Basic Training

Complete the mandatory basic first aid and Red Cross principles training (3-day program).

4

Start Serving

Get assigned to your chosen program area and begin your volunteer service with YRC!
